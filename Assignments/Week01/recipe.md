# Steamed Eggs (계란찜)
This is a simple Korean steamed egg dish
![Steamed Eggs](https://drivemehungry.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/korean-steamed-eggs-gyeran-jjim-3.jpg)

## Ingredients
- 3 eggs
- 100 ml of water
- 3 pinches of salt
- 3 pinches of pepper
- Sesame oil
- Tree onion

## Instructions
1. Break 3 eggs in a bowl
2. Mix the eggs and add salt and pepper
3. Steam for 7 minutes in the microwave
4. Drip one spoon of sesame oil
5. Finish with pinch of chopped tree onion
