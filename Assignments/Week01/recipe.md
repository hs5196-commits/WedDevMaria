# Steamed Eggs (계란찜)
## Description
This is a simple Korean steamed egg dish called gyeran-jjim. The eggs are gently steamed until soft and fluffy, creating a warm and comforting texture. It is commonly enjoyed as a side dish with rice in Korean home cooking.

## Ingredients
- 3 eggs
- 100 ml of water
- 3 pinches of salt
- 3 pinches of pepper
- Sesame oil
- Tree onion

## Instructions
1. Break 3 eggs in a bowl
2. Mix the eggs and add salt and pepper
3. Steam for 7 minutes in the microwave
4. Drip one spoon of sesame oil
5. Finish with pinch of chopped tree onion
