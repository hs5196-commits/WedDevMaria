Steamed Eggs (계란찜)

Soft and fluffy Korean steamed egg dish

Description

This is a simple Korean steamed egg dish called gyeran-jjim. The eggs are gently steamed until soft and fluffy. It is commonly eaten as a side dish with rice in Korean home cooking.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 100 ml of water
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Sesame oil
  • Green onion

Instructions

  1. Break 3 eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add water, salt, and pepper and mix well.
  3. Steam in the microwave for 7 minutes.
  4. Drizzle sesame oil on top.
  5. Finish with chopped green onion.

Tip: Do not overcook. Steamed eggs should be soft and slightly jiggly.
